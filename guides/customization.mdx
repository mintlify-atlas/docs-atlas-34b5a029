BBPlayer offers extensive customization options for appearance, playback behavior, lyrics display, and more. This guide covers all settings to help you personalize your experience.

## Appearance Settings

Access appearance customization at **Settings** → **外观** (Appearance).

### Player Background Style

Choose how the player screen looks:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Background Settings">
    In Appearance settings, find **选择播放器背景样式** (Select Player Background Style).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose a Style">
    Select from:
    
    - **渐变** (Gradient): Dynamic color gradient extracted from album artwork
      - Uses the `@bbplayer/image-theme-colors` package
      - Extracts dominant colors and creates a smooth gradient background
      - Updates automatically when track changes
    
    - **默认背景** (Default Background): Standard Material Design 3 background
      - Uses theme colors from React Native Paper
      - Consistent with app-wide color scheme
      - Better for battery life
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Gradient backgrounds are visually striking but may use slightly more battery. Switch to default background if you prefer a cleaner look or want to conserve power.
</Tip>

### Now Playing Bar Style (Android Only)

Customize the mini player that appears at the bottom of the screen:

- **悬浮** (Float - Default): Floating bar with rounded corners and elevation
  - Sits above content with subtle shadow
  - More visually distinct
  - Better for one-handed use

- **沉浸** (Immersive): Bar integrated into the bottom edge
  - Blends seamlessly with content
  - More screen space for content
  - Cleaner, minimalist appearance

### Audio Spectrum Visualizer

Enable real-time audio visualization:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Spectrum">
    Toggle **显示音频频谱** (Display Audio Spectrum) in Appearance settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Grant Microphone Permission">
    On Android, you'll be prompted to grant microphone permission.
    
    <Note>
      The microphone is used only to analyze audio for visualization - no audio is recorded or stored.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Visualizer Active">
    When enabled:
    - Album cover becomes circular
    - Spectrum bars animate around the cover in real-time
    - Colors sync with the audio's frequency spectrum
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Accordion title="How does the spectrum visualizer work?">
  The visualizer uses the device's audio processing to analyze frequency data in real-time. It requires the `RECORD_AUDIO` permission to access the audio output stream and generate the frequency spectrum visualization.
</Accordion>

## Theme and Colors

BBPlayer uses **Material Design 3** with dynamic theming:

### System Theme

The app automatically follows your device's light/dark mode setting:

- **Light Mode**: Clean white backgrounds with dark text
- **Dark Mode**: OLED-friendly dark backgrounds with light text

<Note>
  BBPlayer uses React Native Paper's `MD3DarkTheme` and `MD3LightTheme` with automatic switching based on `Appearance.getColorScheme()`.
</Note>

### Dynamic Colors

Colors adapt based on album artwork:

1. When you play a track, the app extracts the dominant color palette from the cover
2. This palette influences:
   - Gradient backgrounds (if enabled)
   - Accent colors in the player UI
   - Button tints and highlights

<Tip>
  For the best visual experience, use high-quality album artwork with distinct colors.
</Tip>

## Playback Settings

Access at **Settings** → **播放** (Playback).

### Audio Quality

Select preferred streaming quality:

1. **高** (High): Best audio quality, higher data usage
2. **中** (Medium): Balanced quality and data usage
3. **低** (Low): Lower quality, data saver mode

<Note>
  Quality settings apply to streaming only. Downloaded tracks maintain their original quality regardless of this setting.
</Note>

### Repeat and Shuffle

Control playback behavior:

- **Repeat Modes**:
  - Off: Play through playlist once
  - All: Loop entire playlist
  - One: Repeat current track indefinitely

- **Shuffle**: Randomize track order
  - Toggle in the player controls
  - Shuffle state persists across app restarts

### Playback History

Toggle **发送播放历史** (Send Play History):

- **Enabled**: Your listening history is tracked and synced
  - Enables personalized recommendations
  - Syncs play counts across devices
  - Powers "Recently Played" features

- **Disabled**: No history tracking
  - More private listening
  - No cross-device sync
  - Recommendations less personalized

### Danmaku (Bullet Comments)

For Bilibili videos with comments:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Danmaku">
    Toggle **启用弹幕** (Enable Danmaku) in Playback settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjust Filter Level">
    Use the **弹幕过滤等级** (Danmaku Filter Level) slider:
    - 0: Show all comments
    - 5: Filter moderate spam/low-quality comments
    - 10: Show only high-quality/popular comments
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View During Playback">
    Danmaku comments scroll across the player screen synchronized with the video's timeline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Danmaku adds a fun, interactive element to music videos but may be distracting for audio-only listening. Try filter level 7-8 for a good balance.
</Tip>

## Privacy and Data

Access at **Settings** → **通用** (General).

### Data Collection

Toggle **启用数据收集** (Enable Data Collection):

- **Enabled**: Anonymous usage analytics are collected
  - Helps improve app performance
  - Identifies common crashes and bugs
  - No personal information is collected

- **Disabled**: No analytics sent
  - Completely private usage
  - Developers have less insight into issues

<Note>
  Data collection uses Sentry for crash reporting. Only app version, device model, OS version, and crash stack traces are collected - never your music library or listening habits.
</Note>

### Debug Logging

Toggle **启用调试日志** (Enable Debug Logs):

- **Enabled**: Detailed logs written to file
  - Useful for troubleshooting issues
  - Logs stored in app's document directory
  - Can be viewed in Settings → Logs

- **Disabled**: Minimal logging
  - Better performance
  - Less storage usage

<Tip>
  Enable debug logs only when troubleshooting. Logs can grow large over time and should be cleared periodically.
</Tip>

## Account Settings

### Bilibili Account

Manage your Bilibili login:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to Bilibili">
    Go to Settings → General → **Bilibili 账号** (Bilibili Account).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Use your Bilibili account or scan QR code to authenticate.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sync Features">
    Once logged in, you can:
    - Access private playlists
    - Sync favorites from Bilibili
    - Enable "Watch Later" list
    - Comment on videos (if implemented)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Accordion title="How are Bilibili credentials stored?">
  BBPlayer stores Bilibili cookies in encrypted local storage using MMKV. The cookies are used to authenticate API requests but are never sent to third-party servers. You can clear them anytime from Settings → Account → Logout.
</Accordion>

### BBPlayer Account (Cloud Features)

For playlist sharing and cloud sync:

1. Register/login in Settings → General → **BBPlayer 账号**
2. Your account enables:
   - Shared playlist subscriptions
   - Cross-device playlist sync
   - Backup of playlist metadata

<Note>
  BBPlayer account is optional. All core features work without registration.
</Note>

## Advanced Settings

### Storage Management

View and manage app storage:

- **音频缓存** (Audio Cache): Downloaded tracks
- **歌词缓存** (Lyrics Cache): Cached lyric files
- **封面缓存** (Cover Cache): Album artwork
- **应用数据** (App Data): Database and settings

Actions:
- **清除歌词缓存** (Clear Lyrics Cache): Delete all lyric files
- **清除封面缓存** (Clear Cover Cache): Remove album art (re-downloads as needed)
- **清除应用数据** (Clear App Data): ⚠️ Resets app to factory state

<Tip>
  Clearing caches is safe and can free up storage. The app will re-download content as needed. Only "Clear App Data" deletes your playlists and settings.
</Tip>

### Update Settings

Configure app updates:

- **自动检查更新** (Auto Check Updates): Check for new versions on startup
- **仅 Wi-Fi 更新** (Wi-Fi Only Updates): Download updates only on Wi-Fi
- **加入测试计划** (Join Beta Program): Get early access to new features

### Network Settings

- **仅 Wi-Fi 播放** (Wi-Fi Only Playback): Prevent streaming over cellular
- **预加载下一首** (Preload Next Track): Buffer upcoming track for seamless transitions
- **网络超时** (Network Timeout): Seconds to wait for network requests (default: 30)

## Importing/Exporting Settings

### Export Settings

1. Go to Settings → Advanced → **导出设置** (Export Settings)
2. Select export location
3. Settings saved as JSON file

Exported settings include:
- All preferences from `Settings` object
- Theme choices
- Playback configurations
- Privacy settings

### Import Settings

1. Go to Settings → Advanced → **导入设置** (Import Settings)
2. Select previously exported JSON file
3. Confirm import to restore settings

<Tip>
  Export your settings before reinstalling the app or switching devices to quickly restore your preferences.
</Tip>

## Resetting to Defaults

To restore all settings to factory defaults:

1. Go to Settings → Advanced → **重置所有设置** (Reset All Settings)
2. Confirm the action
3. App will reset to default configuration

<Note>
  This only resets preferences. Your playlists, downloads, and library are not affected.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Settings not saving">
  If your settings changes don't persist:
  
  1. Check available storage (low storage can prevent writes)
  2. Force close and reopen the app
  3. Clear app cache in system settings
  4. Check file permissions in Android settings
  5. As a last resort, reinstall the app
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Theme not switching automatically">
  If light/dark mode doesn't follow your system:
  
  1. Verify system dark mode is enabled/disabled
  2. Force close the app completely
  3. Check for app updates (may be a bug)
  4. Some Android skins require explicit app restart
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Spectrum visualizer not working">
  Common issues:
  
  1. Microphone permission not granted - check app permissions
  2. Android audio policy restrictions - try rebooting device
  3. Bluetooth audio may not support spectrum - switch to device speaker
  4. Some Android ROMs restrict audio access - check manufacturer forums
</Accordion>

## Best Practices

1. **Experiment with themes**: Try both gradient and default backgrounds to see what you prefer
2. **Battery optimization**: Disable spectrum visualizer and reduce motion for longer battery life
3. **Network awareness**: Enable Wi-Fi only playback to avoid mobile data overages
4. **Regular cache clearing**: Clear lyrics/cover cache monthly to free up storage
5. **Backup settings**: Export settings after extensive customization
6. **Privacy balance**: Enable data collection to help development, but disable if privacy is critical
7. **Debug logs for support**: When reporting issues, enable debug logs first to provide detailed information
